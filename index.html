ABOUT US

Let’s do good, is a digital platform that connects people from all walks of life to organisations in need. Our members can donate, join our volunteer, social change and civic actions campaigns to make real world impact on causes they are passionate about. Let’s do good encourages people to take action through organising, mobilising, educating, and advocating so that under privileged people will have the chance not only to survive, but to thrive.
